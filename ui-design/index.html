Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on EDSM?
Click the Sign Up button on the homepage and follow the instructions to register your account.
How do I buy stocks?
Navigate to the Market page, select a stock, and click the Buy button. Enter the number of shares and confirm your purchase.
How do I contact support?
You can contact support by emailing support@edsm.et.

Troubleshooting Tips

I can't log in to my account.
Make sure your email and password are correct. If you forgot your password, use the 'Forgot Password' link to reset it.
The site is not loading properly.
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact support.
My transaction failed. What should I do?
Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue continues, contact our support team for assistance.
